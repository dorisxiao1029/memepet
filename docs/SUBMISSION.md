## Vision

MemePet turns the wallet from a static address into a living companion. Meme coin trading is emotional, social, and chaotic; the interface should reflect that. Instead of another execution bot or risk dashboard, MemePet gives traders a pet that hatches from wallet behavior, grows through market interactions, and reacts to every win, loss, buy, and exit.

The long-term vision is a playful AI-native wallet layer where each wallet has a personality, memory, and emotional presence. Your on-chain behavior becomes a living identity.

## Is this BUIDL an AI Agent?

**Yes.** MemePet is an AI agent because it perceives context, maintains state, reasons with user and market data, and takes actions inside the product loop.

### Agent Capabilities

- **Perception:** wallet behavior, Trading DNA, user goals, conversation history, market context, and trade pulse events.
- **Memory:** persistent local pet state including XP, level, vitals, mood, goals, wallet DNA, and chat history.
- **Reasoning:** AI-generated personality, contextual market commentary, trading-friendly emotional responses.
- **Action:** updates vitals, mood, XP, goals, messages, and optional on-chain identity state.
- **Autonomy:** wakeup endpoint can proactively generate re-engagement messages after inactivity.

### What is MVP vs Future

- MVP: wallet DNA, AI pet generation, trade pulse reactions, market feeding, companion chat, mint/share flows.
- Future: real-time BSC wallet event listener, precise PnL analysis, database-backed persistence, wallet signature auth.

## Why four.meme / BSC

four.meme and BSC represent fast-moving meme trading culture. MemePet is designed for that exact environment: quick discovery, emotional volatility, community-driven narratives, and on-chain behavior that reveals trader personality.

## Demo Flow

1. Open landing page.
2. Use Wallet Hatch or Quick Adopt.
3. Pet hatches into the cockpit.
4. Show Pet Status: XP, Energy, Satiety, Meme.
5. Trigger Live Trade Pulse: Green / Red / Ape In / Exit.
6. Feed Market.
7. Open small companion chat.
8. Show Mint / Share.

## Suggested Submission Answer

MemePet is an AI agent and living meme-trading companion. It reads wallet behavior to create Trading DNA, hatches a personalized pet, and maintains state across XP, level, mood, goals, and vitals. The pet reacts to market context and trade events, celebrates wins, comforts losses, and can optionally mint an on-chain identity. The MVP uses deterministic trade pulses for a stable judging demo, while the production path is real-time BSC wallet event ingestion.
