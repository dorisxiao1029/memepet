# MemePet Demo Video Script

## Target Length

90 seconds

## 0:00-0:08 - Hook

Most crypto tools treat your wallet like a spreadsheet.

MemePet treats it like a living personality.

Your wallet hatches a meme-trading pet that grows with your on-chain behavior.

## 0:08-0:20 - Problem

Meme coin trading is emotional. Traders ape in, celebrate green candles, cope with red ones, and move fast through community narratives.

But most tools are cold: charts, alerts, dashboards, and bots.

They miss the human loop.

## 0:20-0:35 - Solution

MemePet is an AI companion for meme coin traders.

It reads wallet behavior, creates Trading DNA, and hatches a pet with personality, goals, XP, energy, satiety, meme score, and mood.

It does not block degen behavior. It stays beside the trader like a small companion window.

## 0:35-0:48 - Demo Step 1: Hatch

We start with Wallet Hatch.

The app analyzes BSC-style wallet behavior and turns it into pet DNA: trading style, risk rhythm, meme activity, and starter vitals.

The result is a personalized pet, born from the wallet.

## 0:48-1:02 - Demo Step 2: Cockpit

After hatching, the user lands in the meme trader cockpit.

The pet stays alive through XP, Energy, Satiety, and Meme score.

Goals, wallet DNA, care actions, and identity live in one unified experience.

## 1:02-1:17 - Demo Step 3: Live Trade Pulse

Now we trigger trade pulses.

If the user apes in, the pet gets excited.

If the trade goes green, it celebrates and gains XP.

If it goes red, it comforts the trader without stealing the steering wheel.

## 1:17-1:26 - Demo Step 4: Feed + Chat

The market itself can feed the pet.

Hot tokens and sentiment become energy, commentary, and mood changes.

The chat stays small, like a desk-side companion, instead of taking over the screen.

## 1:26-1:30 - Close

MemePet makes wallets feel alive.

It is not just another trading bot. It is your on-chain companion for the meme coin journey.

## AI Agent Answer For Voiceover Or Submission

MemePet is an AI agent because it perceives wallet and market context, remembers pet state and goals, reasons through personality-driven prompts, and acts by updating mood, XP, vitals, messages, and optional on-chain identity state.
