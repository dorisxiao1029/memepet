# MemePet Pitch Deck Narrative Plan

Audience: four.meme / BSC hackathon judges, builders, and ecosystem partners.

Objective: Explain MemePet quickly as a hackathon-ready AI agent product: a wallet-born meme-trading companion that reacts to trade events and market context.

Narrative arc:
1. Hook: Your wallet hatches a living meme-trading pet.
2. Problem: meme trading is emotional, but tools are cold.
3. Solution: wallet DNA creates an AI companion.
4. Product loop: hatch, pulse, feed, chat, mint/share.
5. AI agent reasoning: perception, memory, reasoning, action, autonomy.
6. Tech and roadmap: what exists now and what comes next.

Visual system: dark meme trader cockpit, neon green/pink/yellow, terminal cards, pet orb, ticker motifs, simple editable diagrams.

Slide list:
1. Title / one-liner
2. Problem
3. Product Vision
4. Core Demo Flow
5. AI Agent Architecture
6. Hackathon MVP vs Roadmap

Editability plan: all slide copy, cards, diagrams, labels, and roadmap items are editable PowerPoint objects. The project logo is embedded as an image asset.
